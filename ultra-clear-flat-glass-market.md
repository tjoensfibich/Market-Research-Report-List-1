<p><strong>The Ultra Clear Flat Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra Clear Flat Glass market is primarily divided into two types: Rolled Glass and Float Glass. Rolled Glass is produced by shaping molten glass between rollers, resulting in a thickness that varies with the roll gap, often used in applications requiring less precision. In contrast, Float Glass is created by floating molten glass on molten tin, producing a uniform thickness and superior clarity, making it ideal for high-quality applications like windows and displays. Each type serves distinct market needs.</p></p>
