<p><p>The single-sided masking tape market is classified into three main adhesive types: silicon-based, acrylic-based, and rubber-based adhesives. Silicon-based adhesives offer high-temperature resistance and are ideal for specialty applications, while acrylic-based adhesives provide excellent UV resistance and durability, making them suitable for long-term use. Rubber-based adhesives are known for their strong initial tack and versatility, often used in general purpose tasks. Each adhesive type caters to specific needs, influencing the choice of masking tape in various applications.</p></p>
