<p><p>The Direct Copper-Plated Ceramic Substrate market primarily encompasses two types: Al₂O₃ (Aluminum Oxide) and AlN (Aluminum Nitride) ceramic substrates. Al₂O₃ substrates are well-known for their excellent electrical insulation and mechanical strength, making them suitable for a range of electronic applications. AlN substrates, on the other hand, offer superior thermal conductivity and are ideal for high-power applications requiring efficient heat dissipation. Both types are integral to advanced electronic packaging and contribute to improved performance in electronic devices.</p></p>
