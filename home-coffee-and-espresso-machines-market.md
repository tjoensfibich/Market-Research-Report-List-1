<p><p>Home coffee and espresso machines are categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require users to control all brewing variables, allowing for a hands-on coffee-making experience. Semi-automatic machines automate some processes, like water temperature and pressure, while still requiring user intervention for grinding and tamping. Fully automatic machines offer complete automation, from grinding beans to brewing and frothing milk, ideal for convenience seekers who desire quality coffee with minimal effort.</p></p>
