<p><strong>The Aluminium Oxide Nanoparticles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>α Crystal</li><li>β Crystal</li><li>γ Crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminium oxide nanoparticles are categorized based on their crystal structures: α, β, and γ. The α crystal form is the most stable and widely used, offering high thermal and chemical resistance, making it ideal for ceramics, abrasives, and catalysts. The β crystal form is less common, with unique electrical properties, often used in specialized applications like sensors. The γ crystal form is highly reactive, with a large surface area, making it suitable for adsorbents, catalysts, and coatings. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on its structural and functional properties.</p></p>
