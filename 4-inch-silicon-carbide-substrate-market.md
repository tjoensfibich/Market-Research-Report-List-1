<p><p>The 4-inch silicon carbide substrate market primarily consists of two types: conductive and semi-insulated substrates. Conductive substrates facilitate efficient electrical conduction, making them ideal for high-power applications such as power electronics and RF devices. In contrast, semi-insulated substrates possess a degree of electrical isolation, which reduces parasitic capacitance and enhances performance in sensitive applications like high-frequency circuits. Both types cater to the growing demand for high-efficiency, thermal stability, and robustness in advanced semiconductor devices across various industries.</p></p>
