<p><p>Stone Plastic Composite (SPC) flooring is a type of rigid core flooring made from a combination of natural limestone powder, polyvinyl chloride (PVC), and stabilizers. SPC flooring is designed to mimic the appearance of natural materials like wood or stone while offering enhanced stability and ease of maintenance.</p><p>The Stone Plastic Composite Flooring Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for durable and cost-effective flooring solutions.
