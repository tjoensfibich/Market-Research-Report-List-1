<p><p>The polycrystalline silicon photovoltaic module market is divided into one-sided and double-sided types. One-sided modules generate electricity from sunlight received on one surface, making them cost-effective and suitable for typical installations. In contrast, double-sided modules capture sunlight from both sides, enhancing energy output, particularly in reflective environments like snow or sand. This dual absorption capability offers higher efficiency and return on investment, appealing to those looking for increased performance in diverse settings. Both types cater to varying consumer needs and installation scenarios.</p></p>
