<p><ul><li>Welded Tube</li><li>Seamless Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel tubes market comprises two main types: welded and seamless tubes. Welded tubes are made by rolling steel strips into a cylindrical shape and welding the seam, offering cost-effectiveness and versatility for various applications. Seamless tubes are manufactured by extruding solid billets, resulting in a uniform structure with no seams, making them stronger and ideal for high-pressure or high-temperature environments. Both types cater to industries like construction, oil and gas, automotive, and infrastructure, with their selection depending on specific performance and budget requirements.</p></p>
