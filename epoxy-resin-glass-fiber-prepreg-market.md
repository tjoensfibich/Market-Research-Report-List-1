<p><p>The epoxy resin glass fiber prepreg market is categorized by curing temperatures: low, medium, and high. Low-temperature curing prepregs typically cure at temperatures around 60-80°C, allowing for easier handling and processing in delicate applications. Medium-temperature curing, at 120-150°C, balances performance and processing speed, suited for various industrial uses. High-temperature curing prepregs, cured at 180°C or above, offer superior thermal stability and mechanical properties, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive applications where extreme conditions are encountered.</p></p>
