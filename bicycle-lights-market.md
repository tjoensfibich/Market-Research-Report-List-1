<p><p>The bicycle lights market primarily consists of two key types: headlights and taillights. Headlights are designed to illuminate the path ahead, ensuring visibility during low-light conditions and enhancing safety for cyclists. They come in various brightness levels and mounting options. Taillights, on the other hand, focus on improving visibility from behind, alerting motorists to the cyclist's presence. Both types are crucial for nighttime riding and are available in various styles, including LED, flashing, and rechargeable options.</p></p>
