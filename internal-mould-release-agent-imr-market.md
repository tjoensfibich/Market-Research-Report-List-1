<p><p>Internal Mould Release Agents (IMRs) are specialized formulations used to facilitate the easy removal of molded parts from molds in various manufacturing processes. The market for IMRs is primarily divided into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based IMRs contain organic solvents, providing excellent release properties and potentially lower application costs, but may raise environmental concerns. In contrast, water-based IMRs are more environmentally friendly, offering safer handling and reduced VOC emissions, making them increasingly popular in eco-conscious industries.</p></p>
